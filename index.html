Stately Story of Our Ancestral Spices

At Shivansh Restro, we don’t just cook; we resurrect the culinary codices of royal Indian empires once served inside sandstone fortress chambers of Kashmir, Awadh, and New Delhi. Founded under the guidance of Patron Shivansh Agrawal, our kitchen is governed by traditional techniques where coal tandoor hearths are fed real clay coals and spices are custom crushed on ancient millstones daily by Chef Vineet Rawat.
